How Our Image Compressor Works

Our advanced image compression algorithm analyzes your images and applies optimal compression settings to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust compression settings (or use our recommended defaults)
  3. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  4. Download the optimized image with reduced file size

All processing happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can compress as many images as you want without any limitations.

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can choose the output format or let our tool automatically select the best format for your image.

How much can I reduce my image file size?

Depending on the image and settings, you can typically reduce file size by 50-90% while maintaining good visual quality.

Yes, our compressed images are fully compatible with all websites, social media platforms, and email services.
